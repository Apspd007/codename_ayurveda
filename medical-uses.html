Medical Uses


Ashwagandha

It is also known as Withania somnifera. It used for stress relief, improving immunity, and promoting overall wellness



Turmeric

It is also known as Curcuma longa. It used for anti-inflammatory and antioxidant, used for treating digestive and skin disorders



Brahmi

It is also known as Bacopa monnieri. It used for improving memory and cognitive function



Aloe vera

It is also known as Aloe barbadensis. It used for skin care, digestive issues, and promoting overall health



Neem

It is also known as Azadirachta indica. It used for anti-bacterial and anti-inflammatory, used for treating skin conditions and promoting oral health



Tulsi

It is also known as Holy basil or Ocimum sanctum. It used for treating respiratory and digestive problems, as well as stress relief



Licorice

It is also known as Glycyrrhiza glabra. It used for treating respiratory and digestive issues, as well as reducing inflammation



Haritaki

It is also known as Terminalia chebula. It used for improving digestion, promoting weight loss, and reducing inflammation
